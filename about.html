Hello! I’m currently a junior at Carnegie Mellon studying Communication and Environments Design, with a minor in Human-Computer Interaction.

My interest in design stems from my passion for storytelling and creating memorable experiences for others. I’m excited to explore how visual design can be used in a variety of ways, from efficient user experiences to eye-catching and impactful branding systems.

In my spare time, I love to try out new games, watch movies on Netflix or in theaters, and explore cities looking for new places to eat (I try my best to document my boba adventures)!
