Hiragana Book
Book Design

Since I was in elementary school, I've been interested in Japanese culture and language. I wanted to create a book for myself that could combine my love for Japan and the hiragana letterforms.

I handbound and side-sewed the pages, which were printed on rice paper. Each page consists of a hiragana character and a city in Japan that starts with it.
