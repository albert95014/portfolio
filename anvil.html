Anvil

Anvil is a Bay Area-based startup whose products utilize AI to improve and consolidate paperwork processes for a variety of industries. I worked with them to redesign a collection of pages on their website.

Anvil Homepage →

Overview

I was tasked with redesigning the pricing and a suite of product pages for Anvil's website. The deliverables were to include mocks for tablet and mobile, as well as responsive components that fit with Anvil's existing style guide and design library.

Timeline

The pricing page was done over the course of 3 weeks, whereas the product pages took around a month from ideation to final designs.

Pricing Page

Updates to the pricing page included adding two new plans and adjusting the corresponding feature matrix accordingly. The mocks included not only the responsive states but examples of scroll behavior and edge cases for overflowing content.

I worked closely with the marketing lead to review the existing page and review ways of re-organizing the information.

Product Pages

I continued working with Anvil to redesign their product pages, starting with a module for previewing language snippets and linking to a documentation-style layout. The goal was to create a template webpage showcasing their existing SDKs and corresponding products, able to be re-used as more languages and features were developed.

Designing the suite of product pages included how the website handles code snippets, card components, and the corresponding hover states.
